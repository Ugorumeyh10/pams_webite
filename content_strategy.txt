Content Strategy Document for Pringles Brand

Objective

The objective of the content strategy is to enhance brand awareness,
engage the target audience, and drive product consideration for Pringles
through digital media and marketing.

Target Audience.

- Millennials and Gen Z aged 18-34.

- Snack enthusiasts.

- Young professionals/ working class.

- Digital-savvy individuals who enjoy convenience and unique flavors.

Key Message

Pringles offers a wide range of delicious and innovative flavors,
providing a fun and convenient snacking experience for any occasion.

Content Pillars

- Flavorful Experiences: Showcasing the diverse flavors and unique
combinations that Pringles offers.

- Snack Hacks: Sharing creative ways to enjoy Pringles, such as recipes,
pairings, and DIY snack creations.

- Pop Culture Connection: Leveraging pop culture moments and
partnerships to engage the target audience.

- Showing the different occasions or events Pringles can be enjoyed
with. Whether it’s a picnic, movie night or game night e.t.c

Contents Formats

- Social Media: Engaging and visually appealing posts on platforms like
Instagram, TikTok, and Twitter.

-Video Content: Short snack-hack tutorials, flavor exploration videos,
and behind-the-scenes footage, feature videos.

- Blog and Articles: Longer-form content featuring snack recipes, fun
facts, and lifestyle tips.

Content Calendar

- Weekly: Flavor of the Week social media posts highlighting a specific
Pringles flavor.

- Weekly: mini web series of people telling their Pringles story.

- Bi-weekly: Snack Hack videos demonstrating creative uses for Pringles.

- Monthly: Blog articles featuring snack recipes, pop culture tie-ins,
events and brand stories.

SEO Optimization

- Keyword research to identify popular snack-related search terms.

- Optimized content titles, meta tags, and descriptions for increased
search visibility.

Display Ads/Google Ads

- Running promotional sales with mobile ads and display ads.

Influencer Partnerships

- Collaborate with popular food influencers to create engaging content
featuring Pringles.

- Host giveaways and challenges to encourage user-generated content and
brand advocacy.

Social Media Advertising

- Targeted social media ads to reach the desired audience segments.

- Promote new flavors, limited editions, and special offers to drive
product consideration.

Measurement and Analysis

- Track social media engagement, website traffic, and conversions.

- Analyze audience demographics, content performance, and campaign ROI.

- Adjust strategy based on data insights to optimize results.
